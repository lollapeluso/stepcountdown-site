Latest Release

Version 1.2

Released July 2026

Current version

Version 1.2 brings Nudge to more Apple devices, adds more ways to personalize your daily reminders, and delivers smarter walk recommendations to help you stay on track throughout the day.

+ Expanded Support

  • Added backwards compatibility with iOS 17
  • Added backwards compatibility with watchOS 10
  • Added support for all Apple Watch display sizes
  • Refined layouts to make better use of every Apple Watch screen

↑ Smarter Coaching

  • Added more optional Daily Nudge times throughout the day
  • Added more notification options to better fit your routine
  • Improved walk recommendations based on your remaining steps
  • Refined guidance to suggest more practical walk durations

✓ Improvements

  • Improved notification scheduling and delivery
  • Refined Apple Watch layouts and responsiveness
  • Improved performance across supported devices
  • Bug fixes and additional interface refinements

Previous Release

Version 1.1

Released July 2026

Previous version

Version 1.1 introduced the new Nudge identity, improved the first-time experience, expanded step history, and refined performance throughout the iPhone and Apple Watch apps.

+ New

  • New Nudge name, branding, and visual identity
  • Welcome experience for first-time users
  • Expanded daily and weekly step history
  • Additional historical data collection for future insights

↑ Improved

  • Refined Apple Watch complication experience
  • Improved navigation and usability
  • More reliable Apple Health step updates
  • Refined countdown and walking-time calculations

✓ Fixed

  • Improved synchronization between iPhone and Apple Watch
  • Improved reliability when refreshing step counts
  • Performance and responsiveness improvements
  • General bug fixes and interface refinements

Initial Release

Version 1.0

Released June 2026

Initial release

The first public release of Nudge introduced a simpler way to understand what remains between you and your daily step goal.

  • Daily step countdown
  • Apple Watch companion app
  • Finish By daily planning
  • Walking-time estimates
  • Ahead, behind, and on-pace guidance
  • Apple Health integration
  • Apple Watch complications
  • Daily and weekly history
  • Step-based walking plans
  • Indoor and outdoor walk options
  • Optional progress reminders
  • Daily goal celebrations
