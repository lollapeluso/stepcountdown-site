Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Step Countdown respects your privacy.

Step Countdown uses Apple HealthKit step count data to show your daily step progress, steps remaining, and estimated walking time needed to reach your goal.

Your HealthKit data is used only on your device. Step Countdown does not sell, share, or transmit your HealthKit data to third parties.

Step Countdown does not use your data for advertising or tracking.
