Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

Your Privacy

Nudge ("Nudge", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy.

Nudge is designed to help you reach your daily step goal while keeping your personal data private.

Health Data

With your permission, Nudge uses Apple HealthKit step count data to display your daily progress, steps remaining, estimated walking time, and other coaching features.

Your HealthKit data remains on your device. Nudge does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share your HealthKit data with us or any third parties.

Advertising and Tracking

Nudge does not use your data for advertising, analytics, tracking, profiling, or marketing purposes.

Future Features

If future versions of Nudge introduce optional cloud features or services that require sharing data, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.
